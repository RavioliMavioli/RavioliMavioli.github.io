Terms of Service

General


  • Female characters are prefered, I can draw male too but the result will be not as good as female.
  • Only 1 character.
  • Strictly No Echi and or NSFW.
  • No humongous oppai and or thicc thighs.
  • Not accepting Chiby, Mecha, Furry, Old Character, Weird Fetishes (sorry, I didn't mean to kinkshame you), Complex Poses, Complex Background, Controversial Topic, and Realistic Style.
  • No deadlines or rushes.

Payment


  • Only pay when I accept your commission. Any payment beforehand is considered as donation.
  • Upfront payment:

    • First, 30% of the total price for the sketch.
    • Second, 40% of the total price for the base shading.
    • Third, 30% of the total price for the final render.

  • Payment via PayPal (International) or BRI/DANA/OVO (Local).
  • I may cancel or refuse your commission due to any reason.

Final Artwork


  • Final artworks will be sent after you send the full payment.
  • Final artworks will be sent in the form of PNG at a full resolution with the source file.
  • Final artworks will be sent via email.
  • Final artworks will be uploaded to my Instagram, Pixiv, DeviantArt, and other social media at 30% resolution with the addition of watermarks under clients permission.

Copyright


  • Both the artist (me) and the client (you) should retain the Copyrights.
  • You are free to redistribute the artworks.
  • Unless you choose the "Commercial" category, DO NOT sell or make any profit from the artworks.
  • Please do not claim that you drew the commissioned artworks.
